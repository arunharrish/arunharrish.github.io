I'm a graduate student at Georgia Institute of Technology pursuing my MS in Mechanical Engineering, maintaining a perfect 4.0 GPA. My work sits at the intersection of high-fidelity simulation, machine learning, and design optimization.

From conjugate heat transfer on rotary engine coolant systems at LiquidPiston, to aerodynamic load prediction for tower cranes using RANS CFD — I build models that are both accurate and actionable.

I'm particularly drawn to problems where surrogate modeling and DOE can replace expensive simulation loops — reducing computation time without sacrificing fidelity.
